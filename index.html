What If Calculator

A compound interest calculator that displays the potential savings of daily, weekly, or monthly in both the longterm and shorterm.

Built with the D3.js library and Javascript, this application has a smooth interface and makes calculating potential savings fun. Many people are surprised to see what saving just $5 or $10 per day over the course a lifetime will return when compound interest is involved.
